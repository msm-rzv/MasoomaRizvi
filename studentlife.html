STUDENT BUDGET PLANNER

SEPTEMBER 2023 - DECEMBER 2023

"A student-first budgeting application designed to help post-secondary students take control of their finances through personalized planning, expense tracking, and goal-setting."

TOOLKIT

Figma | wireframing & prototyping
UX Research | user personas & surveys

READ-TIME

5 minutes

"Student life is financially challenging, our app helps simplify budgeting by giving students a clear, customized financial view tailored to their lifestyle, goals, and habits."

DISCOVERY

Through surveys and research methods (including observational data and Google Form responses), we identified recurring issues students face regarding financial management.

“Balancing transportation, food, and tuition costs is overwhelming.” – Maddie, 20

“I want to budget and set savings goals, but I don’t know where to begin.” – Vladimir, 21

“Being a student on residence, it's hard to track where all my money goes.” – Sarah, 22

“The gym and healthy food options are limited and costly on campus.” – Richard, 19

Needs Assessment

Our findings highlighted major financial, health, and academic pressures faced by students:

  1. Lack of tools for organizing and visualizing monthly expenses and savings.
  2. Minimal guidance on how to manage financial goals effectively.
  3. Inadequate campus support for wellness and budgeting balance.

DEFINE

Key Pain Points Identified:

  1. Poor financial planning tools for university-specific needs.
  2. Difficulty maintaining budgets across categories like food, rent, tuition, and transportation.
  3. Lack of visual progress or reminders toward financial goals.

Goals:

  1. Design a budgeting app tailored to students’ lifestyles.
  2. Offer personalized goal setting, progress tracking, and financial calendars.
  3. Ensure ease-of-use, clear data visualization, and a motivating UI.

Mission Statement

"To empower students to take control of their finances through an accessible, intuitive budgeting tool designed around their academic lifestyle and personal goals."

Design & Branding Decisions

We opted for a friendly and intuitive layout using calming blues and vibrant accents to build trust and engagement. Icons and simple charts help users navigate their budgets quickly. A circular pie chart offers instant feedback on spending, and toggle switches help users customize goals or view progress in graphs.

DELIVER

After prototyping, we shared our design with 4 target users including commuters, international students, and on-campus residents. Feedback informed several updates:

Key Feedback & Iterations

“I want the app to remember past purchases and autofill categories.”

“Can I track income in the same chart as expenses?”

“The progress graph is helpful, but monthly views would be better.”

“It’s motivating to have savings goals visually shown on a timeline.”

Final Updates

  • Added a savings progress bar to the goals section.
  • Included monthly filtering in graphs and progress views.
  • Allowed repeated purchase templates to speed up expense tracking.
  • Created toggle options for light/dark mode for comfort and accessibility.
